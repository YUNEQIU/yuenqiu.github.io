团队介绍


团队隶属于上海教育软件发展有限公司设计部门，目前成员为十位技术精湛且经验丰富的设计师，常年深耕于教育相关领域，全心全意为企业和客户提供优质的美术设计解决方案。

The team belongs to the Design Department of Shanghai Educational Software Development Co., Ltd. It currently consists of ten highly skilled and experienced designers who have been deeply engaged in the education sector for many years, dedicated to providing high-quality art and design solutions for businesses and users.
